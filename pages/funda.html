Focus + Passion = Impact

Goal

Funda gave a workshop for the students from the minor WebDesign. The Workshop consisted of building a features with a relative large group of people. We actually looked more at the product instead of code. And importantly, don't stay at one problem and touch as much product because, you'll learn more. You can be more of use by a bigger variety then just one, but also for yourself, dare to challenger yourself. Start from the bottom, small and compact and end up big.

Reflection workshop

Because the workshop was in VueJs and a big team for a small assignements working in Scrums. It was hard to fulfill the feature. We had just one hour and 30 minutes for it and that was for building and communicating with other groups. A big part of the group never heard of VueJS, so it was more looking it up the actually building for the case. But it was a challenge, for communicating with other groups and watching merge conflicts happening. Total chaotic, but fun It was short but fun case to be working with. And the group that helped us are very interesting to listen to. Very informative and calm.
